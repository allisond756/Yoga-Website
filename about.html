A Little Bit About Us

First established in 2012 and orginally was just a group of four friends wanting to learn and share their knowledge of yoga with one another. As time passed friends of the group also wanted to join on the groups yoga sessions. From there the group grew and grew until they bought their first location in downtown Houston. Bringing in trained professionals that could offer a larger variety of courses to the students of Paradise Yoga. There are now four different locations all through out the Houston metro area and looking to expand to cities like Austin and San Antonio. From beginners yoga to hot yoga to an advanced class Yoga Paradise has the class for you!
